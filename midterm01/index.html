About HOHO Dessert Shop

HOHO dessert is my shop in China. Professional production, management of all types of orthodox Hong Kong dessert. Hong Kong Man Kee Dessert Group has developed from an early family sugar water shop into a chain dessert shop since 1995 when the first Man Kee dessert was set up in Sai Kung District of Hong Kong. Manji dessert to "Mango class halberd" and "sugar dessert" series is particularly prominent.In order to ensure the quality of food and enhance the operation efficiency, the Group has its own central food manufacturing plant, fruit processing warehouse and logistics transport fleet. All production, delivery and sales involved in the operation are handled by us. Accumulated years of operating experience, our business model has become systematic, including cost control, material selection, production process, quality control, logistics and transportation and product sales, and so on, every detail is meticulous; Products are constantly updated and improved to ensure the best quality products for customers and maintain the competitiveness of the market. HOHO Dessert's most famous products: mango halberd, durian halberd, poplar mandew, snow black pearl. Additional, the heart dessert with 3 kinds of Chinese style is very famous: raw "sesame paste, walnut dew, apricot benevolence tea", welcome everybody to taste.
